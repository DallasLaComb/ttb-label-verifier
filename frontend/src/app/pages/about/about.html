About

ColaReady is a small tool that helps beverage alcohol producers and importers catch label problems before submitting a Certificate of Label Approval (COLA) application to the Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB).

Getting a COLA application rejected over a typo, a missing government warning, or a mismatched alcohol content statement costs time and delays getting product to market. This tool reads the label artwork you plan to submit and compares the printed text against the values in your application, flagging anything that doesn't match or needs a closer look.

How it works

  1. Enter your application data - brand name, class/type, alcohol content, net contents, producer name and address, and country of origin (if imported) - and upload a PNG of your label artwork on the Verify page.
  2. An AI vision model reads the printed text from the label image.
  3. Each field is then compared against your application data using fixed TTB labeling rules - for example, alcohol content is checked within the regulatory tolerance, and the Government Warning is checked against the federally mandated wording and formatting, not against application data.
  4. You get a field-by-field report marked Match, Mismatch, or Needs Review, which you can print or save as a PDF.

A note on accuracy

This tool is a pre-submission helper, not a substitute for TTB review. It can miss issues or misread small print, so always double-check your label against the official requirements before submitting.
