About

ColaCheck is a small tool that helps beverage alcohol producers and importers catch label problems before submitting a Certificate of Label Approval (COLA) application to the Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB).

Getting a COLA application rejected over a typo, a missing government warning, or a mismatched alcohol content statement costs time and delays getting product to market. This tool reads the label artwork you plan to submit and compares the printed text against the values in your application, flagging anything that doesn't match or needs a closer look.

How it works

  1. Upload a PNG image of your label artwork on the Verify page.
  2. The image is sent to an AI vision model that reads the printed text.
  3. Key fields - brand name, class/type, alcohol content, net contents, producer address, country of origin, and the government warning - are extracted and compared against your application data.
  4. Any mismatches or fields that need a closer look are highlighted for review.

A note on accuracy

This tool is a pre-submission helper, not a substitute for TTB review. It can miss issues or misread small print, so always double-check your label against the official requirements before submitting.
